Into the Floo

My name is Mona Morsy, founder of Into the Floo, and I've been under the spell of the Harry Potter Trading Card Game since I was 14 years old. It all started with a blister pack gifted to me by my mother. I remember it being marked down with a bright orange sale sticker on it, which must have been from Kmart, Kohl’s or Target. Inside was a Base Set booster pack that, once opened, unlocked a world I never wanted to leave. I carefully tucked those cards into a binder, flipping through them often like pages of the Harry Potter books. That simple gift became the seed to a lifelong passion.
While my collection journey has had its ebb and flow, I’ve been fully committed for the past 10 years in tracking down rare cards, ultra-limited items and even one of a kind artifacts from the games vault. Along the way, I’ve had the privilege of meeting some great people who worked on the game and had first hand experience in bringing this beloved game to life!
Into The Floo is a way for me to keep this magic alive and share it with fellow collectors, players and fans of the game who still believe in the wonder of it all.
