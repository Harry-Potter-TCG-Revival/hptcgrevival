Welcome new players!


The resources below will walk you through learning how to play the game. The first set of resources will walk you through playing the game, building a deck, and the rules. Then you can decide whether you want to play Classic or Revival and join our discard to find games and talk about decks!

We recommend starting with these resources. Each button will take you a video or a useful resource!






Classic Format

For those who want to play the game as it was discontinued in 2002 by WOTC. This includes only the original 5 sets and follows Pojo's rulings and the original rulebook.

Revival Format

For those who want to play the game with the original sets and fan-made releases, a banned & restricted list, a clarified rule book, mulligan, and a sideboard.

If we can provide any other new player resources, email us at harrypottertcgr@gmail.com!
