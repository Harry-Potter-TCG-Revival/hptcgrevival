Accio About
The HPTCG Revival is a non-profit effort to rebuild the community and grow the game. We'll be doing this with tournaments, competitive play, strategy discussion, and with the creation of fan-made sets.
You can view a history of the HPTCG and of HPTCG Revival here.
If you have any questions, please email us at harrypottertcgr@gmail.com or join our Discord and call the #prefect tag.
