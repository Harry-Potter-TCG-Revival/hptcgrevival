Ways to play!

There are multiple ways to play the HPTCG. Whether you enjoy constructed or draft, Classic or Revival, there's a way of playing you'll enjoy.

Tournaments!


We host tournamnets every 3 months on our discord server. Tournaments are free to play and use Untap to play.

Drafting


Released in summer 2025, we have a solo drafter on our website Accio.cards and are running draft tournaments. We also utilize drafting in Table Top Simulator for those who own the program.
Refer to our draft rules and guide for specific draft rules.

Dueling Club!


We host fun Dueling Club events on our discord server multiple times a month. These are low stakes times when people gather to duel! See a full monthly schedule on our discord server.
