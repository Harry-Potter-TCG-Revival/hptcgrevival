Formats

There are currently two formats to play HPTCG, Classic and Revival. Please see the differences below.

Classic
  • WOTC Sets (BS, QC, DA, AAH, COS)
  • Rules as left by WOTC
Revival
  • WOTC sets (BS, QC, DA, AAH, COS)
  • All Revival sets
  • Banned and Restricted List
  • Updated Rulings and Rulebook
  • Errata List
  • Mulligan
  • Sideboard
